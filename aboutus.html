What We Care About


We want to make sharing money simple, as simple as giving cash to a friend. We always give for a reason, whether its to share a coffee, buy a book, send a gift, give pocket money to kids or buy stuff online ... every action of giving has an emotional value attached to it.


fastacash is a simple and effective way to send and receive money across social networks in a transient and frictionless way.


We are all used to posting photos, videos and messages to our social networks. fastacash enhances that experience by adding the ability to send secure transactions with a simple link, which can be moved to any social platform. Now you can send and receive money across your chosen social channel, such as Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, Viber, Whatsapp or any other social network. We hope you will enjoy using fastacash as much as we are enjoying designing and building it.
